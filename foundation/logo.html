Logo

The GR monogram is a minimal, geometric personal identity mark. It uses a dot separator between initials to create a clean, modern, and memorable mark.

Primary Logos

Black & White

Use on light backgrounds where full color is unavailable.

Reverse

White on dark backgrounds — sidebar and hero sections.

Color

Full color version — preferred on white and light backgrounds.

Color Reverse

Full color on brand blue — used in homepage hero.

Logo Variations

Variation 1 — Horizontal lockup with wordmark

Variation 2 — Stacked layout for compact spaces

Variation 3 — Monogram only, minimal mark

Variation 4 — Badge / stamp format for social profiles
