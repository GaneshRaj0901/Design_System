Color

The GR color palette is built around trust, clarity, and confidence. Each color has a specific role and should be used consistently across all touchpoints.

Primary Blue
#0E6DF9

Brand color, CTAs, links, active states

Accent Gold
#F5A623

Highlights, hover accents, tags

Dark Navy
#1A1A2E

Backgrounds, sidebar, headings

White
#FFFFFF

Page backgrounds, reversed text

Color Use Cases

1. Primary Blue on White

Used for navigation links, call-to-action buttons, and interactive elements. The high contrast between #0E6DF9 and white ensures WCAG AA compliance and strong visual hierarchy.

View Project
2. Dark Navy + White Text

Used for the sidebar, hero sections, and full-bleed backgrounds. The deep navy (#1A1A2E) grounds the layout and makes white text highly legible at all sizes.

This is sidebar text on dark navy. Clean and readable.

3. Accent Gold for Highlights

Used to indicate active navigation states, hover accents, and keyword tags. Gold draws the eye without competing with the primary blue — it signals selection and energy.
